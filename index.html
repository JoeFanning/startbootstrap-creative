Domain Name

Website Domain Name

Your domain name is like your house address.
When someone wants to find your website online they type in your domain name:

www.mywebsite.com

It's the address used online to find your website.
How do I get a domain name?

To get a domain name for your website, you must register one online.

Don't worry i will do this for you.

Website Hosting

Website Hosting

Website hosting is a server/computer where your website lives online.
When someone wants to find your website online they type in your website name(domain name).

www.mywebsite.com

Your domain name is used to find your hosting computer/server where your website lives. Then that hosting computer/server sends your website to whatever persons computer that typed in you domain name.
How do I get a website hosting?

To get website hosting you must rent space on a computer/server online for your chosen period of time. Different online companies provide this services.

Don't worry i will do this for you.
